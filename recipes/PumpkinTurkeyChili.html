Pumpkin Turkey Chili

This delicious turkey pumpkin chili is the perfect combination of flavorful chili spices, black beans, and lean ground turkey.

If you have ever needed some savory ways to include more pumpkin in your recipes, this is such a great option. It gives the chili a rich texture and deep flavor that is so good!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 dash salt
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until tender.
  2. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown; drain.
  4. Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin. Season with chili powder, pepper, and salt. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
